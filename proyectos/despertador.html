Alarm clock

2008

For reasons unkown a bird perches itself on the exterior of a window still, tapping the window pane intermittently in anticipation of the pending alarm clock on the interior. Over a period of a week the bird repeated the same pattern of behaviour. After trying
Is heard at the very end, so it goes. Once recorded never returned.
