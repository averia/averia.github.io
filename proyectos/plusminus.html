2010

electronic board, seven segments display, proximity sensor and beacon light.

Dimensions variable.

A numeric counter of 3 digits calculates and displays the distance in centimeters between the viewer and the work itself. A beacon light illuminating when viewers are to close.

programming: albert molins.
